Research Overview

I am a PhD student in Applied Mathematics at Politecnico di Torino. My work explores how topological and structural constraints shape representation, capacity, and generalization in both biological and artificial intelligence.

Applied Topology

Utilizing TDA and discrete calculus for networks and higher-order topological structures.

Cognitive Science & AI

Mathematical modeling of cognitive constraints and tradeoffs in representational learning.

Neural Loss Landscape

Investigating the theoretical properties of loss landscapes, focusing on symmetries and learning dynamics.

Higher-order Networks

Studying scales and topology in higher-order networks and their associated dynamics.
