Research

I study the mathematical foundations of learning and complex systems. My work focuses on how topological and structural constraints shape representation, capacity, and generalization in both biological and artificial intelligence.

Applied Topology

Topological data analysis and discrete calculus for networks and higher-order networks.

Cognitive Science & AI

Mathematical modeling of cognitive constraints and the tradeoffs inherent in representational learning.

Neural Loss Landscapes

Theoretical properties of the loss landscape: symmetries and their interplay with learning.

Higher-order networks

Scales and topology in higher-order networks and dynamics.
